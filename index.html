Our Mission

ABBA's Kingdom PMA Donation Program operates as a **Private Membership Association (PMA)** committed to advancing the Gospel of Jesus Christ and demonstrating ABBA's love. This initiative aligns with Messiah's teachings to bring practical aid to widows, orphans, and the poor while proclaiming Jesus Christ crucified, buried, and raised on the third day.

Demographics and Kingdom Impact

Target Demographics:

  • Primary: Faith-based individuals aged 25–55 focused on Kingdom growth (58%).
  • Secondary: Those seeking alignment with Biblical principles of giving (42%).

Global Reach: Through scalable efforts, our vision includes reaching up to 0.5% of the world's population—approximately 12.5 million participants—within four phases of growth.
