Impact Summary

This program will be transformative, bringing practical benefits to families, individuals, and entire communities while simultaneously fueling Kingdom initiatives. The funding generated will be directed toward empowering people to:

  • Pay bills and improve financial stability
  • Save for education, weddings, and major life milestones
  • Purchase homes, vehicles, and invest in long-term assets
  • Build businesses, adopt children, and transform their communities
  • Support local and global Kingdom initiatives, including homeless shelters, orphan care, and missions

As we continue to scale and expand, the vision and impact of ABBA's Kingdom Donation Program will continue to grow beyond anything we can imagine, fulfilling the mission of Life and Life More Abundantly (John 10:10) and continuing to demonstrate the expressed generosity of Jesus to the world.

Partnering with ABBA to build His Kingdom on Earth. All glory to God.
