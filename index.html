ABBA's Kingdom Donation Program

Empowering Generosity Through Holy Spirit-led Vision

Introduction

ABBA’s Kingdom Donation Program is a revolutionary initiative designed to restore Holy Spirit generosity through biblical principles of sowing and reaping. Rooted in John 10:10 and Ephesians 3:20-21, the program embodies the abundant life promised by Jesus while delivering tangible impact across multiple demographics. This program operates on a two-tier structure: Main Link Donations and Affiliate Partnerships, both designed to maximize Kingdom impact while empowering individuals and communities globally.

Phase-by-Phase Growth and Financial Analysis

Phase 1: Foundation (First 30 Days)

Goal: 1,000 donors contributing a one-time $1,222 donation. This phase establishes the foundation for the program.

Total Donations: $1,222,000

Distribution: 58% ($708,760) supports NLC Kingdom projects, and 42% ($513,240) goes toward Co-Creator initiatives.

Affiliate Impact: 400 affiliates generate $439,920 in earnings, empowering them to impact families and communities.

Phase 2: Expansion (Months 2-12)

Goal: Scale to 125,000 donors, resulting in $152,750,000 in total donations.

Distribution: 58% ($88,595,000) for NLC Kingdom and 42% ($64,155,000) for Co-Creator.

Affiliate Impact: 62,500 affiliates generate $137,475,000 in earnings, further multiplying the program’s global reach.

Phase 3: Acceleration (Year 2)

Goal: Reach 1,250,000 donors, resulting in $1,527,500,000 in donations.

Distribution: 58% ($885,950,000) for NLC Kingdom and 42% ($641,550,000) for Co-Creator.

Affiliate Ripple Effect: 750,000 affiliates contribute $1,374,750,000 in earnings.

Phase 4: Transformation (Years 3-5)

Goal: Expand to 4,250,000 donors globally, resulting in $5,191,750,000 in total donations.

Distribution: 58% ($3,010,215,000) for NLC Kingdom and 42% ($2,181,535,000) for Co-Creator.

Affiliate Ripple Effect: 2,975,000 affiliates generate $4,672,575,000, fostering a global movement of Holy Spirit generosity.

Demographic Analysis

Target demographics include:

  • Affiliate/Side Hustle Market: Over 95 million individuals globally seeking passive income opportunities.
  • High School Students (13-18 years): Over 600 million youth worldwide represent a critical demographic for inspiring Holy Spirit-led purpose.
  • Faith Communities: 2.5 billion Christians globally with potential to sow into Kingdom purposes.
  • Families and Businesses: Focused on breaking generational poverty and fostering Kingdom-minded stewardship.
