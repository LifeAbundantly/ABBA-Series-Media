ABBA's Kingdom Donation Program

Empowering Generosity, Transforming Communities, and Advancing the Kingdom of God

Overview

The ABBA's Kingdom Donation Program is a Spirit-led movement of generosity rooted in Jesus Christ and Him crucified, buried, and resurrected. This program combines the joy of giving with passive income opportunities, fostering transformation through the principles of God's Kingdom.

Demographics and Reach

Global Christian Population: Over 2.5 billion Christians globally.

Projected Reach: A 0.5% penetration means reaching over 12.5 million people, contributing over $15 billion annually to Kingdom missions.

Target Demographics:

  • Primary: Active Christians, aged 25–55, financially capable (58%).
  • Secondary: Faith-based individuals and passive income seekers (42%).
  • Online Income Demographic: Engaging those seeking opportunities in affiliate marketing and passive income sectors, projected to grow to $20 billion by 2025.
