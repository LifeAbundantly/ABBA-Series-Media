ABBA's Kingdom Donation Program

Growth Analysis and Impact Projections

Program Overview

The ABBA's Kingdom Donation Program is designed to harness the power of Holy Spirit-inspired generosity, transforming lives by merging faith-driven giving with modern affiliate systems. Starting with 1,000 donations in the first 30 days, the program aims to achieve 17% penetration across multiple demographics globally.

Demographic Breakdown and Timeline

The program targets key groups:

  • Christian Adults (25–55): 1 billion potential participants globally.
  • High School Students (14–18): 600 million globally.
  • Young Adults (18–24): 400 million globally.
  • Passive/Online Income Seekers: 200 million globally.

Timeline and milestones:

  • Month 1: 1,000 donations achieved. Probability: 95%.
  • Year 1: 0.5% penetration (~11 million participants). Probability: 80%.
  • Year 3: 5% penetration (~55 million participants). Probability: 65%.
  • Year 7: 17% penetration (~374 million participants). Probability: 45%.

Donation Split Structure

Transparent and Kingdom-focused:

  • Main Link: 58% to NLC Kingdom, 42% to Co-Creator.
  • Affiliate Link: 90% to Affiliate, 9% to NLC Kingdom, 1% to Co-Creator.
