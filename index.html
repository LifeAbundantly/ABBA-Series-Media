Vision and Purpose

ABBA's Kingdom Donation Program is more than a platform—it’s a Kingdom-driven movement to empower individuals and restore communities through generosity. By uniting spiritual and physical impact, this program mirrors Jesus' multiplication miracles and the Spirit of generosity.

This initiative ensures that every donation, every affiliate action, and every tool is purposed to make the generosity of Heaven known on Earth.
